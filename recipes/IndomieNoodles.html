Indomie Instant Noodles

Description:

Nothing quite like feeling lazy to cook and realizing you have a pack of Indomie instant noodles in your drawer.

Ingredients:

  • A pot with water.
  • 1 or 2 packs of Indomie noodles, depending on your hunger level.
  • 2 eggs.
  • Condiments found in the Indomie package (dried onion, seasoning oil, chili powder, seasoning paste).
  • 1 tsp of olive oil.

Preparation:

  1. Fill a pot with water and let it boil.
  2. While you wait for the water to boil, put 1 tsp of olive oil in a frying pan and start heating it (low heat recommended).
  3. Fry the eggs. You can leave them on one side for 4 minutes and then flip them, if you're not a sunny side up type of person.
  4. Once the water starts boiling, put the dried noodle blocks in the pot and leave them for 2 minutes.
  5. Drain the noodles and apply all the condiments provided in the package, mixing them evenly in the portion.
  6. Serve with the fried eggs on the noodles.
