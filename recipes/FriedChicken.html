Fried chicken

Description:

For when you're craving KFC but your wallet is giving you a look of disapproval when you think of ordering. This recipe will take approximately 25-30 minutes of preparation time.

Ingredients:

The below ingredients listed are for two servings:

  • Chicken thigh fillets (400g)
  • Seasoned flour (100g)
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tbsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp curry
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups of frying oil
  • 1 tbsp of chili powder (optional)

Preparation:

  1. Take out the chicken fillets and cut them into smaller pieces.
  2. In a bowl, mix the seasoned flour with the other dry ingredients.
  3. In a separate bowl, crack the egg open and use a fork to batter it.
  4. Take the chunks of chicken and coat them with the battered egg.
  5. Coat again the chicken chunks with the mix of dry ingredients.
  6. Put the oil in the frying pan and heat it up.
  7. Put the chicken pieces in the pan and let each side fry for 8 minutes on low heat.
